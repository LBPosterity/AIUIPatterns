Complete Result Regeneration

Overview

AI usualy provides a single resutl per prompt, this pattern simply re submits and regenerates the entire previous prompt result.

User Archetype

This pattern is more applicable for the context of the content rather than the users ability. It's primarily a quesiton of the users expectaiton of the systems flexibility. More technically proficient users ay want more granular control.

Considerations

This pattern is optimised for succinct prompt with shorter results. Consider if the results your users are generating are more subjective & if full regeneration could assist ideation.

Next consider if regenerating the results require edits to the initial prompt or users coud benefit from contectually relevant pre canned prompts such as "make more succinct or make less technical".
