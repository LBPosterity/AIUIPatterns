Cloze Passage Variables

Overview

A cloze passage pattern leverages two aspects unique to AI, which is the conversational nature of the technology, favoring the input of desired outcomes & context over direct commands. As well as the natural language approach of the models themselves.

User Archetype

A common exercise in english education, a Close Passage with blank spaces in a regular sentence is an intuitive & farimiliar format for users.

Being that it's input is tightly scoped by the sentence & the options provided this pattern fits best for beginner users who need clear guidance.

Considerations

An effective Close Passage is intuitive for users to complete. the two things to consider are A. The total amount of answers needed, these should be low, in the 3-6 range & B. The volume of available answers per question. A question with a large amount of options doesn't suite this pattern & slows uers down.

Consider, do I only need to ask a few questions, are the answers generally simple & can I present this in a coherant parragraph?
